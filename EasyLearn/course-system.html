Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my certificate?

Once you complete 100% of the course modules and pass the final quiz with at least 60%, your certificate will be automatically generated. You can download it from your Dashboard or the Course Page.

Are the courses free?

Yes! We offer a wide range of free courses to help you start your journey. Premium courses with advanced projects and mentorship are also available.

Can I access course materials offline?

Currently, video lessons require an internet connection. However, you can download source code, notes, and cheat sheets for offline use.

Is there a time limit to finish?

No, all our courses come with lifetime access. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the material whenever you like.

Earn valuable certifications

Earn credentials that show employers what you can do. Our platform offers certification exams at different skill levels, and you'll see your results right away. These industry-recognized certificates help you stand out and get ahead in your career.

Each of our certificate courses includes the exam.

When you enroll in one of our certificate courses, you'll receive all the materials and guidance you need to pass.
